Privacy & Data Use Policy

At TUI Academy, we value your privacy and are committed to safeguarding your data. By logging in, you agree to the following:

  • What We Collect: Your Discord ID, username, and email address.
  • Why: To track your training progress, verify your account, and assign roles on our Discord server upon course completion.
  • Storage: This data is securely stored using Firebase Admin SDK, protected and encrypted.
  • Who Can Access: Only TUI Academy management team members responsible for training and moderation.
  • Retention: Your data is kept only for as long as you are actively participating in TUI Academy.

If you do not agree with these terms, please do not log in.
